Our Story

Three years ago, my life changed when I was diagnosed with celiac disease. Like many others with this condition, I constantly struggled to communicate my dietary needs—especially when traveling or dining out. Language barriers made this challenge even more daunting.

I realized that while society evolves, the tools available to celiac patients have remained stagnant. Explaining celiac disease is difficult enough to someone who's never heard of it, but it becomes nearly impossible when they speak a different language.

This personal struggle sparked the creation of Celio—a web application designed to help people with celiac disease and gluten sensitivities safely communicate their dietary needs in multiple languages.
